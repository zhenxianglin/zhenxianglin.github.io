Selected Projects

3D Vision Grounding on Large-Scale Scenes
Research Assistant's Project, 2021-2023. [arXiv]

We introduced the task of 3D visual grounding in large-scale dynamic scenes based on natual linguistic descriptions and online captured multi-modal visual data, including 2D images and 3D LiDAR point clouds. We presented a novel method, WildRefer, and proposed two novel datasets focusing on large-scale human-centric daily-life scenarios.

Multi-label Long-tailed Distribution Image Classification
Master Research Porject, 2021. [Report]

We researched SOTA methods for solving the long-tailed Distribution problem, and conducted experiemnts on different factors including data resampling, loss re-weighting and feature extractor. Finally, we combined the advantages of different methods and achieve a higher performance.

Plant Image Process Based on Computer Vision
Computer Vision Porject, 2020. [Report]

We searched both tradional and deep learning computer vision algorithms on detection and segmentation. For detection and semantic segmentation, we use traditional methods including HOG and CIELAB color space. For instance segmentation, we train a UNet.
